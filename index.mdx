Ancra is a Dedicated Virtual Account (DVA) infrastructure API built for developers who need to embed Nigerian banking capabilities into their products. These docs walk you through everything from provisioning your first virtual account and handling inbound payment webhooks, to initiating outbound bank transfers, managing a double-entry ledger, and keeping your books in sync with periodic reconciliation sweeps.

## Get Started in Four Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an API Key">
    Sign in to the Ancra dashboard and generate a secret API key for your environment (sandbox or production). Store it securely — you'll pass it as a Bearer token on every request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Customer">
    Register each end-user as an Ancra **Customer** with their KYC details (name, BVN, tier). Ancra enforces Tier 1–3 limits automatically so you stay compliant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provision a Virtual Account">
    Call `POST /accounts` with the customer ID to instantly generate a dedicated Nigerian bank account number. The account is unique to that customer and ready to receive NIP transfers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive a Payment">
    When a payer sends funds to the virtual account number, Ancra credits the customer's ledger and fires a signed webhook to your endpoint. Verify the HMAC-SHA256 signature, then fulfil the value in your app.
  </Step>
</Steps>
